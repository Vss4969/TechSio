
How to get into our application:

  a)  Clone our codes into your htdocs folder and you are good to go.

  b)  Entry point to our website  src/index.php.

Steps to start with your account on TechSio site:

  a)  Use the signup option to create your new account on the site and also upload a photograph from your device as a profile photo. 
      Input required: User-name, E-mail ID, Password, Profile photo.

  b)  Remember email-id and password for future reference.
